We'll ask if you want to notify:

  • tax, benefits and entitlements (including State Pension)
  • local council services, such as Council Tax, Housing Benefit and Blue Badge passport, driving and vehicle services
  • armed forces pension and compensation schemes
  • public sector pension providers (if they worked in the public sector, for example, in the NHS, local government or civil service)

If they died in a hospital, care home, nursing home or hospice we’ll also ask:

  • for the name and address of that institution
  • if they were there for more than 28 days before they died
