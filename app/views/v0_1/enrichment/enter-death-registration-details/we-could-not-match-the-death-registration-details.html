What you can do

Check that the reference number has not expired. The reference number to use Tell Us Once stops working 28 days after it was issued.

If your reference number has expired, you will need a new one to use Tell Us Once. To check if you can get a new reference number contact the register office where the death was registered.

If your reference number is valid

Wait 20 minutes and enter the death registration details again.

Or, you can contact us to complete Tell Us Once by phone.
