Their National Insurance number

If you know the National Insurance number of the person who died:

  • it can help the organisations and departments you notify, such as DWP and HMRC, to find their records quicker
  • we can check if they had any public sector pensions (for example, if they had a pension with the NHS or civil service)

The National Insurance number is on their National Insurance card, payslip or P60. If they got state pension or benefits, it may be on a benefit letter or bank statement.
