{{ serviceName }} prototype

Designs and design ideas for the Tell Us Once service.

What's included

You can use this prototype to explore designs for:

  • the public facing Tell Us Once journey (enrichment)
  • specific pages for the agent facing Tell Us Once journey (enrichment)
  • the service used by registrar to enter death registration details (capture)

The prototype includes full journeys with branching on some questions to show different scenarios. You can also see standalone pages without follwing an entire journey.

How to use the prototype

Interact with the prototype in the same way users do. Most pages don't require you to enter any information to continue to the next page. However, by answering questions you will get a more realistic reflection of how users will see it.

No information you enter when using the prototype will be saved. We recommend using made up information, especially if you are taking and sharing screenshots or sharing your sceen on a call.

End to end journeys

These prototype journeys include new designs and may be different from the live service. They do not use production code.
