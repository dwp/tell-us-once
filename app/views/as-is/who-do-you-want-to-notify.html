By selecting 'yes', you give permission for us to tell that department about the death.

You can save or print a list of the organisations you have notified at the end.

Tax, benefits, entitlements including State Pension

If they received State Pension or other DWP benefits such as Attendance Allowance.
What is a DWP benefit or entitlement?

If you're not sure if they receive a DWP benefit, select yes. DWP can then check and update their records.

The most common DWP benefits and entitlements include:

  • Attendance Allowance (AA)
  • Disability Living Allowance (DLA)
  • Emplyoment Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Income Support
  • Jobseeker's Allowance
  • Pension Credit
  • Personal Independence Payment (PIP)
  • State Pension
  • Universal Credit
  • Winter Fuel Payment

Other DWP benefits and entitlements include:

  • Bereavement Support Payment (BSP)
  • Incapacity Benefit
  • Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit
  • Maternity Allowance
  • Widowed Parent's Allowance (WPA)
For personal and individual tax records such as Pensions, Pay As You Earn (PAYE), Self Assessment or Self Employment.

What is an HMRC benefit or credit?

  • Tax-Free Childcare
  • Help to Save

Passport and driving

Provide a valid British passport number. You can enter an expired passport number if there is no current passport and it was issued after 1989. You don't need to return their passport, cut off the top right-hand corner so it cannot be used fraudulently.
You will need the driving licence number of the person who's died. You can enter an expired licence number. You do not need to return their driving licence, but you should cut off the top right-hand corner so it cannot be used fraudulently.
When DVLA is notified of the death, the vehicle tax will no longer be valid. If the vehicle is going to be driven, or is parked on a public road, it will need to be re-taxed. If there are more than five vehicles you will need to contact DVLA directly.

Armed Forces Pensions and Compensation Schemes

If they had an Armed Forces Pension Scheme (AFPS 75, AFPS 05, AFPS 15) or a Reserve Forces Pension Scheme in payment when they died.
If they had a War Pension Scheme award in payment when they died. Payable for injury or illness occuring before 6th April 2025.
If they had an Armed Forces Compensation Scheme award in payment when they died. Payable for injury or illness occuring on or after 6th April 2025.

Public Sector Pensions

Check which public sector pensions we can tell

We can only notify these public pensions:

  • NHS Pensions for NHS staff in England and Wales
  • Civil Service Pensions
  • Scottish Public Pensions Agency (SPAA) for teachers and/or NHS
  • Scottish Public Pensions Agency (SPAA) for police and/or firefighters
  • Pension Protection Fund and/or Financial Assistance Scheme
  • Local Government Pension Funds (LGPF)

If they had any other public sector pensions, you will need to notify them yourself.
