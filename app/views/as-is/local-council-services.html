Local council services

Which council was providing most services to the person who died?

For example, council housing, library services, Blue Badge, or paying Council Tax.
If they lived in an area with more than one council, you need to select the borough or district council from the list. We can then work out which council to notify too
If you want to tell more than one council

There may have been more than one council providing services to the person who died. For example, a home address where they paid Council Tax, but a care home where they received library services.

Select the council that was providing most services as that is who we will notify. You will need to contact the other council yourself to tell them about the death
