Before we send the information

The organisations, in the list below, are the ones that you have agreed to being told. They will now be able to update their records and amend benefits, credits and services without asking to see the death certificate.

All the information you have provided will be protected and shared securely. The organisations will only be given the information that will be used by them and will treat your information securely and confidentially. You can learn more about this by reading the organisation's privacy policies.

We will send the information you have provided about yourself, the next of kin or person dealing withthe estarte to the organisations you have chosen to notify. They will contact the appropriate person if they need to.

  • DWP Bereavement Service Team 5
  • HM Revenue and Customs - Personal Tax
  • HM Revenue and Customs - Benefits and Credits
  • Hereford Borough Council
  • Ministry of Defence - Veterans UK
  • HM Passport Office
  • Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency
  • Veterans UK
  • My Civil Service Pension

Further requirements
