Before we send the information

You've given Tell Us Once permission to notify the government departments you have selected about the death. The departments will update their records. They will not need to see the death certificate.

Tell Us Once will share the information you have provided about yourself, the next of kin and person dealing with the estate.

If any of the departments need more information, they will contact the next of kin or the person dealing with the estate.

All the information you have provided will be protected and shared securely. The departments will only be given information that will be used by them and will treat your information securely and confidentially .Find out more by reading the departments' privacy policies.

You told us you want to notify:

  • Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) - Benefits and Entitlements
  • HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) - Personal Tax
  • HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) - Benefits and Credits
  • London Borough of Lewisham Council - Council Tax
  • London Borough of Lewisham Council - Library Services
  • London Borough of Lewisham Council - Blue Badge
  • HM Passport Office (HMPO) - Passport
  • Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency (DVLA) - Driving Licence
  • My Civil Service Pension
