If you notified DVLA

The DVLA will remove the person who died as the registered keeper of the vehicle. The vehicle tax will no longer be valid. You must tell DVLA if you either sell the vehicle or keep it. If you keep it, you will need to tax the vehicle in your own name.

Check if you can get financial help

If your partner has died, and you're under State Pension age, you could be eligible for Bereavement Support Payment. You must have been married, in a civil partnership, or living with a dependent chilld to be eligible.

If you live in England or Wales, you could be eligible for help with funeral costs if you get certain benefits.

You can call the DWP Bereavement Service free of charge and they can check if you're eligible and make an application.
